◆ renderingRangeImage

ORUtils::Image<Vector2f>* ITMLib::Objects::ITMRenderState::renderingRangeImage

Gives the raycasting operations an idea of the depth range to cover.

Each pixel contains an expected minimum and maximum depth. The raycasting step would use this information to reduce the range for searching an intersection with the actual surface. Should be updated by a ITMLib::Engine::ITMVisualisationEngine before any raycasting operation.
